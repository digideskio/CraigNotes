Our Commitment to Privacy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy is posted to explain how we collect and use your information.

How We Use Your Information

We NEVER share ANY of this information with ANYONE but you. No other users can view this information, and we won't share it with any third parties.

We use non-identifying and aggregate information to better design our website and to share with advertisers. For example, we may tell an advertiser that X number of individuals visited a certain area of our website.

Personal Information We Collect

When you login to the website, we collect the following:

  • E-mail address - We don't currently use this, but we have a feature planned which will let you subscribe, if you so desire, to certain updates via e-mail.
  • Username / display name - Used to show you that you are logged in.
  • OpenID Identifier - This identifies the account you use to login to our site.

Our Commitment to Data Security

We only allow users to access our service once they have logged in via OpenID. This means we do not store or even have access to any of your passwords - we entrust the security of that important data to well-known, safe third-party OpenID providers like Google and Facebook.

No other user can access information you enter on our site (ad ratings, notes, etc.).

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is changed, we will post a notice on the login page. We will also provide a summary of any changes, and ensure that the old privacy policy remains available for at least thirty days after the new policy is posted.
